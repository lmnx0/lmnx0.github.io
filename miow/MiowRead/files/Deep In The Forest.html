Deep In The Forest

Status: Ongoing
Author: Gwen Margareth H. Barro
Genre: Thriller, Gory, Horror
Synopsis:
In a forest called Aokigahara, Koichi and his group of friends planned on having a vacation in there. As they arrived at the forest, their camping adventure began. They had fun spending time with each other the first day. But as the moon rose, the forest darkened, and some of them slept, not knowing the danger awaiting them. Unknowingly, it was the night of massacre as many of them will be exterminated in an inhuman way.
